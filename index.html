Brackets Editor: Quick Edit for CSS and JavaScript

No more switching between documents and losing your context. When editing HTML, use the Cmd/Ctrl + E shortcut to open a quick inline editor that displays all the related CSS, LESS and SCSS files, including nested rules. This also works during Live Preview, just click on what you want to change in the Chrome Broswer window, then use Ctrl + E.
